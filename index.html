Easy Cleaning in Just 3 Steps

Dawn Powerwash Dish Spray's innovative formula makes cleaning dishes quicker and easier than ever before. No more soaking. No more scrubbing. Just spray, wipe, and rinse.

How to Use Dawn Powerwash

Step 1

Spray

Simply spray Dawn Powerwash directly onto your dirty dishes. The spray activates on contact with water and begins to break down tough food residues instantly.

Step 2

Wipe

Using a sponge or dishcloth, wipe the surface of your dishes. The powerful formula helps cut through grease and stuck-on food with minimal effort.

Step 3

Rinse

Rinse your dishes with clean water to remove the soap and any remaining food particles. Your dishes will be sparkling clean and ready to use again!

Pro Tips for Best Results

For Tough Grease

For extra greasy pots and pans, spray and let sit for 1-2 minutes before wiping to give the powerful formula time to break down stubborn grease.

Burnt-on Food

For burnt-on food, spray generously, let sit for 5 minutes, then wipe. For extremely stubborn residue, you may need to repeat the process.
