How to Use Dawn Powerwash

This Is Different From Regular Dish Soap

Dawn Powerwash works differently than liquid dish soap. For best results, follow these simple steps.

Step 1

Spray

Apply to dry dishes first - Spray Dawn Powerwash directly onto your dirty dishes. The spray activates on contact with stains and begins to break down tough food residues instantly.

For best results, apply to dry dishes before adding water.
Step 2

Wipe

Wipe, don't scrub - Using a sponge or dishcloth, gently wipe the surface of your dishes. The powerful formula helps cut through grease with minimal effort.

Remember to wipe, not scrub - let the formula do the work!
Step 3

Rinse

Rinse your dishes with clean water to remove the soap and any remaining food particles. Your dishes will be sparkling clean and ready to use again!

Pro Tips for Best Results

For Tough Grease

For extra greasy pots and pans, spray and let sit for 1-2 minutes before wiping to give the powerful formula time to break down stubborn grease.

Remember: Wipe, Don't Scrub

Many consumers mistake wiping for scrubbing. Wiping requires gentle, light pressure - let the formula do the work. Scrubbing is not necessary!

Apply to Dry Dishes First

Always spray Dawn Powerwash on dry dishes first for maximum effectiveness. The formula works best when not diluted with water initially.
